My Story

Several years ago I discovered software development as a bio-science laboratory automation engineer.   And I loved it!   As a creative and artistic person I was particularly drawn to the user interface experience to ensure simple and intuitive access to product functionality and value.   After 13 years in the enterprise software space managing development, product lifecycle and corporate strategy I promptly returned to hands-on development.   My expertise is with the JavaScript technology stack including AngularJS, ExpressJS, NodeJS and MongoDB.   I am also an instructor for the MEAN Stack Web Framework course 30377 at UCSC Extension and enjoy preparing educational videos to help engage others in this exciting and powerfull technology stack.
